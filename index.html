Lisa Gerrard's projects

  • 1981 - present - Dead Can Dance (music group) - singer and composer
  • 1995 - present - Lisa Gerrard (solo project) - singer and composer
  • 1998 - Collaboration with Pieter Bourke (album "Duality")
  • 1999 - Collaboration with Pieter Bourke in writing the musical score for the film "The Insider"
  • 2000 - Collaboration with Hans Zimmer in writing the musical score for the film "Gladiator"
  • 2004 - Collaboration with Patrick Cassidy (album "Immortal Memory")
  • 2005 - Collaboration with Ennio Morricone in writing the musical score for the film "Fateless"
  • 2007 - Collaboration with Klaus Schulze (double-album "Farscape")
  • 2009 - Collaboration with Michael Edwards, Patrick Cassidy, Pieter Bourke and James Orr (album "The Black Opal")
  • 2010 - Collaboration with fellow composer Marcello De Francisci (album "Departum")
  • 2010 - Collaboration with Marcello De Francisci in writing the musical score for the film "Tears of Gaza"
  • 2015 - Collaboration with British composer, songwriter and producer, Chicane (album "The Sum of Its Parts" )
